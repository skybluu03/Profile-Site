A Glimpse Into Me

Hello! I'm Skye.

I'm currently an undergraduate student studying Biotechnology with a focus in Bioinformatics at the University of Houston, with plans to continue into graduate studies. I'm interested in exploring the intersection of biology, technology, and art, and finding ways to bring those fields together through creative projects.

Over the course of my degree, I've filled notebooks and journals with ideas that never made it much further than the page. This website is a place to finally start building some of them, while documenting the process along the way.
